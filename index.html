About Us

Welcome to St. John's HS Kurumannu, Kottayam districts, Kerala (Govt. Aided English and Malayalam medium school under Corporate Educational Agency, Palai Diocese) where every student is valued and empowered to excel. Founded on a rich tradition of academic excellence and community engagement, our school is dedicated to providing a dynamic and inclusive learning environment for students of all backgrounds.

Our school prides itself on fostering a culture of innovation, creativity, and collaboration. With a team of dedicated educators who are passionate about inspiring and guiding students, we strive to cultivate critical thinking skills, instill a love for learning, and nurture the whole child.

At Kurumannu St. John's school, we believe in the importance of character development and social responsibility. Through various extracurricular activities, community service initiatives, and leadership opportunities, we encourage our students to become compassionate, ethical leaders who make a positive impact in the world.

We invite you to explore our website to learn more about our programs, facilities, and vibrant school community. Whether you're a prospective student, parent, or community member, we look forward to welcoming you to the St. John's family.
